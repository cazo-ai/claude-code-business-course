# Competitor Research: 24 Hour Fitness

## Overview
- **Locations:** 280+ clubs (primarily West Coast)
- **Price Point:** $30-$80/month (comparable to FitHub)
- **Brand:** Accessible 24/7 fitness for busy lifestyles
- **Market Position:** Middle-tier, convenience-focused

## Their Rewards/App Features

### 24GO App
- **Workout tracking:** Log cardio, strength, classes
- **Personalized plans:** AI-suggested workouts based on goals
- **Virtual coaching:** On-demand workout videos
- **Check-in tracking:** Visit history and patterns
- **Class reservations:** Book group classes in advance

### What Makes It Work
- **Personalization:** App learns your preferences over time
- **Progress visualization:** Charts and graphs show improvement
- **Variety:** Combines tracking with guidance
- **Integration:** Syncs with Apple Health/Google Fit

## Engagement Tactics
- Weekly progress reports via email
- Achievement notifications
- Personalized workout suggestions
- Class recommendations based on history

## What They Do Better Than Us
1. **Workout guidance:** Actual training plans, not just visit tracking
2. **Progress tracking:** Visual representation of fitness journey
3. **Personalization:** App adapts to individual member behavior
4. **Content variety:** Mix of tracking, coaching, and class booking

## What We Do Better
- Stronger community feel and local events
- Better personal trainer quality
- More welcoming, less corporate vibe
- Group class variety and instructor quality

## Key Takeaway
Their app is a fitness companion, not just a loyalty program. It helps members achieve their fitness goals, which naturally drives engagement.

## Member Quotes (from our surveys mentioning 24 Hour)
> "They actually track my workouts, not just that I showed up"
> "The app suggests what I should do next"
> "I can see my progress over time which motivates me"
