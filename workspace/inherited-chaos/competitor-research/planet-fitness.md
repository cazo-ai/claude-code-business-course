# Competitor Research: Planet Fitness

## Overview
- **Locations:** 2,400+ clubs across US and internationally
- **Price Point:** $10-$24/month (lower than FitHub's $35)
- **Brand:** "Judgment Free Zone" - accessibility and inclusivity
- **Market Position:** Dominance in budget fitness segment

## Their Rewards/App Features

### Planet Fitness App
- **PF Black Card perks** tracked digitally
- **Workout logging** with progress tracking
- **Guest privileges** managed through app
- **Free fitness training** booking
- **Hydromassage/tanning reservations**

### What Makes It Work
- **Gamification:** Achievement badges and workout streaks
- **Challenges:** Monthly themed challenges (e.g., "March Madness Workout Challenge")
- **Community feel:** Share milestones with gym friends (optional)
- **Simplicity:** Easy to understand, low cognitive load

## Engagement Tactics
- Push notifications for new challenges
- Celebrate workout streaks
- In-app rewards unlock (Black Card perks)
- Social features (but not forced)

## What They Do Better Than Us
1. **Clear value:** App features map directly to member benefits
2. **Fun factor:** Challenges feel like games, not homework
3. **Consistency:** Regular new content keeps it fresh
4. **Accessibility:** Simple enough for least tech-savvy members

## What We Do Better
- More personalized service (they're huge and impersonal)
- Better trainer access and expertise
- Community events and local feel
- Higher-quality equipment and facilities

## Key Takeaway
Their app succeeds because it adds genuine value to the membership experience AND makes fitness feel achievable and fun. It's not just visit tracking - it motivates and celebrates.

## Member Quotes (from our surveys mentioning PF)
> "Planet Fitness makes working out feel like leveling up in a game"
> "I like how they celebrate when I hit a streak"
> "The challenges give me something to work toward"
